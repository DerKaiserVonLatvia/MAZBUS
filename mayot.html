Mayot

Details: Artyom Gennadyevich Nikitin (born February 2, 1999, Tyumen),
better known as Mayot, is a Russian hip-hop artist,
a member of the Melon Music musical association.
While studying in elementary school, in the second grade, he began to play the guitar.
Later he learned about rock, which opened the musical world for him in a new way:
everything he heard was at odds with the usual knowledge about music that he received earlier - “solfeggio, musical notation”.
Then he began to study on his own with tutorials on YouTube.
In the 8th grade, I started playing guitar less, as a period of constant parties began.
